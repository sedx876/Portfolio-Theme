ReadrMeetr

I always found that some of the best coversations to be had is over a good book.

So why not bring people together over their love of books.

Users can sign up for an account and create entries for books they have read. They can also peruse a list of books other members have posted and leave a comment about that book.

Users can also find books using "tags" that other users have attached to the book.

Technologies:
  • - Rails
  • - Github OAuth
  • - SQLite
