Strain Domain

With the increased legalization of marijuana, there is a growing need for more research and friendly user apps.

I specifically designed this small app with medical marijuana patients in mind. It can be quite confusing when first entering a medical marijuana regimen. There are literally thousands of strains that effect different people in different ways.

A patient can enter a strain they have tried for their condition. They can log how it effected them and the percentage of THC and CBD the strain contains.

I would like to expand this app by pulling specific strain data through a stable external API in the future.

Technologies:
  • - Rails API Backend
  • - Vanilla JS Frontend
  • - SQLite
  • - Bootstrap
